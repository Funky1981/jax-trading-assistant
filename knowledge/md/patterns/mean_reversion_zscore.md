A standardized deviation from a reference mean (e.g., VWAP, moving average).

## Typical uses
- Mean reversion strategies
- Pair spreads / cointegration residuals
- Risk rebalancing triggers

## Failure modes
- In trends/crashes, z-scores can stay extreme and keep getting worse.
